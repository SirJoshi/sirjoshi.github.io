Covid-19 (The Novel Coronavirus)

Introduction

     Covid-19 is a Virus found to have originated in Wuhan, China. It generates flu-like symptoms but has a chance of generating none at all. Covid-19 has a base mortality rate of about 2% but affects older people much greater than the youth with no recorded deaths of a person under the age of 10 and having seniors claim the large majority of deaths.

Transmission

     The disease transmits from Human to human. It occurs primarily via respiratory droplets from coughs and sneezes within a range of about 6 feet. It can also transmit through Indirect contact via contaminated surfaces. It is unknown if the virus is infectious during the before symptoms appear.

Prevention

     There is no publicly available Coronavirus vaccine but independent studies and testing is currently ongoing. In order to prevent capturing the virus, actively take steps to avoid transmission. Removing your hands, bathing in high alcohol content solutions, and dimensional disassociation are very good ways to prevent getting sick, If you find yourself unable to do the preceding, washing your hands and developing an introverted personality (Social Distancing) will reduce your chances of becoming a statistic.
